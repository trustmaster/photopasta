How to use this extension

  1. Find or create a shared album in Google Photos and copy the share link
  2. Open the share link in an incognito window (to obtain persistent public image URLs)
  3. Click on the photo to open view mode
  4. Click on the full photo to get it in browser's focus
  5. Right click to open context menu
  6. Choose "CopyPasta -> Copy Image Markdown" to copy image shortcode

Troubleshooting

If the context menu doesn't appear or clicking on the menu items doesn't do anything for some reason, reloading the page and trying again typically works.
